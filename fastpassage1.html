Passage - 1

Tina lives at home with her mom and dad. She wants to sail in a ship to see the world. Ships sail on the sea. One day, she gets on a ship. There, she sits on a bench. Tina looks up at the sky and down at the sea. She can even spot a few fish! Now, Tina is hungry. She buys a cheese roll. Tina sits on a chair to eat. She likes the sea, the sun and the fresh air. Tina is happy on the ship. She can't wait to go home and tell her mom and dad about her trip.
